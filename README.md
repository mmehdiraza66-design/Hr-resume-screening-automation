HR Resume Screening Automation
An AI-powered pipeline that automatically scores PDF resumes across 6 dimensions and populates a dashboard.

🎯 Problem It Solves
Recruiters spend countless hours manually reading formatting-heavy resumes just to extract basic qualifications. This manual screening is prone to bias, fatigue, and extreme delays in the hiring pipeline.

⚡ How It Works
A candidate emails their PDF resume or submits it via a form.

n8n extracts the PDF text and sends it to GPT-4 with a strict scoring prompt.

The AI evaluates the candidate across 6 predefined dimensions and outputs structured JSON.

The data is instantly pushed to a Google Sheets dashboard for the HR team to review.

🛠 Tech Stack
Automation: n8n

AI: GPT-4

Integrations: Gmail API, Google Sheets API, PDF Parser

Deployment: Custom Hosted

📊 Results
⏱ Reduced time-to-screen from 5+ minutes per resume to under 10 seconds.

📉 Eliminated 100% of manual data entry for applicant tracking.

🔄 Standardized the evaluation metric, reducing human bias.
